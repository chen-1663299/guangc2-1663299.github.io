What is the theory?

For mental and psychiatric health! Be Happy!

When your anxiety is all-consuming and you're freaking out, having your pet jump into your lap and begin to snuggle can really help bring you back down.

When you have a pet around, dealing with the aloneness becomes a little bit easier. Having another body in the house with you, someone you can talk to in order to ease your nerves, can help so much more than you might think. When you have a pet, someone is always home, so really, you're never actually alone.
